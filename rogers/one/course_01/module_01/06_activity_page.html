Week 2

Activity Drawing your own Geography

For this activity you will need:

  • blank paper you can draw on
  • drawing implements: pencils, pens, crayons, etc.
  • something you can use to time yourself (watch, clock, phone, etc.)

Instructions

Collect all the required items and set your timer for 5 minutes. Then, start the timer and draw whatever images come to mind when you think of your own geography. When the timer is up, select Activity Follow-up below. Also, keep this drawing, because you will be required to post it to this weekâ€™s assessed discussion board. Your artistic ability will not be evaluated!

Activity Follow-up

Reflecting upon what you have drawn, answer the following questions for yourself:

  • Why these images?
  • Why not other images?
  • What does this indicate to you about your own understanding of geography at this point in time?
