Introduction

In this module, we look at various definitions of geography, what this means for our course, and how it relates to us in our everyday lives. We begin to explore how people have related to their environment from the days of cave-dwelling through to today and how our relationships have evolved (both positively and negatively). But it isn’t all about how individuals make choices that affect their planet, it’s also about how populations of citizens (whether towns, nations, or a planet) impact the natural landscape too. Where we live, how, and why, are all important questions to consider when we begin to learn about geography.
