Week 2

Summary

In this module, we have explored what geography means – both in the classroom and beyond. We’ve seen how our evolution as human beings has dramatically altered the landscape and that as our populations have increased, our natural environments have begun to be impacted as well with increasing pressures being applied to our natural resources. These are all important points as we move onto the next module where we will continue on with our understanding of how we got to where we are today. We will look at the impacts of human populations on the environment and how we might be able to make well-informed decisions that help with the sustainability of the planet.
